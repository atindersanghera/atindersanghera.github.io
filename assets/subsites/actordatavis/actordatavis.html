Data Visualisation: Netflix Movies & Actors Narrative Chart

The chart below utilises the D3 visualisation tool with the Netflix and iMDB movies datasets to build a story and is inspired by drzax from ABC News.

It shows popular Hollywood actors & actresses that collaborated for movies over time where data has been subset for Hollywood actors that have been in at least 8 movies.

Each rectangle is a movie ordered by release year and shows the actors that starred in it together.

A blue line indicates an actor with more Fresh movies than Rotten from the Rotten Tomatoes dataset over all the movies they have starred in and a red line is the converse.

The fill signifies how much the movie grossed worldwide with a yellow fill signifying a gross over the average of $129 million.

As we move to the right, it is apparent that red actors tend to have red co-stars.
